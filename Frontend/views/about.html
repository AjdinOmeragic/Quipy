About Quipy

Welcome to Quipy, a meditation and stress relief platform dedicated to helping you manage anxiety, stress, and depression. Our approach is rooted in proven techniques and consistent practice, empowering you to enhance your well-being and transform your life.

Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Ajdin, a third-year student at International Burch University. I created Quipy as part of my university project to support mental health, recognizing its growing importance in today's world.

Our Core Values

  • Empathy: We understand and share the feelings of our users.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and strong moral principles.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek creative solutions for mental well-being.
